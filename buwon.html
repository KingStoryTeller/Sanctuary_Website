Buwan - Final Form

Home Gunpla

This is the project that started it all. Here in San Diego, there is a local store called Gunnzo. It is an awesome store that specializes in selling Gunpla and other cool Japanese imports. After picking up some model kits, the shop owner let me know that they were holding a 30MM contest. I had never worked with this line of model kits before but they looked pretty cool and the price was perfect. I decided to join the contest and this is what the final project turned out to be. The concept is a Mecha Knight from the Moon. Greatly inspired by shows like Aura Battler Dunbine and Panzer World Galient. This is truly a special time in my life that Buwon encapsulates too vividly.
